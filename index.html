Nutritional Benefits

All types of edible mushrooms contain varying degree of protein and fibre. They also contain vitamins B as well as a powerful antioxidant called selenium which helps to support the immune system and prevent damage to cells and tissues. Mushrooms are being increasingly researched and used for their important health benefits whith differenct varieties having different medicinal properties.

How do you store Mushroom

Avoid storing mushrooms in the moisty environment or near foods with strong odor or flavors because they'll absorb them like a sponge, Do not stack other foods ontop of the mushrooms as yuou will bruise them, Most mushrooms hold up better in cold so if you want to store them for more than a week consider freezing them or drying them.

How do you cook Mushrooms

The simple rules to follow when cooking mushrooms are as follows. Cut or tear them to a similar size, Don't crowd the mushrooms in the pan, Salt the mushrooms at the end of cooking and another best thing to do when cooking any type of mushroom is "Keep them dry" since they are spongy they'll absorb a lot of water so try to use as little water as possible.
